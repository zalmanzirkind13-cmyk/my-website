MTF Leather Works is a leather craftsmanship business founded by Moshe Tzvi Fine. The company focuses on producing high-quality, durable leather products with careful attention to detail and design.

Products include wallets, watchbands, tefillin and tallis bags, tzedaka pouches, and other custom leather items. In addition to new products, MTF Leather Works offers repair services for tallis and tefillin bags, as well as leather binding for siddurim.

MTF Leather Works is committed to providing reliable service, fast turnaround times, and strong product quality at competitive prices.
